About Sing-Along Singapore

This web application was created to allow Singaporeans to appreciate the rich Singaporean music culture that has grown over the past 50 years.

We would also like to commemorate Singapore's 50th year of independence, in conjunction with SG50.

To find out more about SG50, click the image below.



SG Heart Map is one of the many other events being conducted to celebrate 2015.
